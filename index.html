Overview

So DEVCU++ is organizing a hackathon in Covenant University this semester, and it's been dubbed HackCU.
At this hackathon, developers, designers and business brains alike will come together, brainstorm solutions to common cross-sectional problems faced in our communities and pitch to business leaders.
The hackathon has been structured to foster a community of developers and business brains in the school community, who would continuously come up with solutions to global issues.
Serious talk aside, there'd be free food and snacks, and you'd get to meet really smart people. We hope you've saved the date already, 'cause we have.
You do have to register ahead of time though, so take a minute and do so, will you?
